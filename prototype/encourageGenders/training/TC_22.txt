We are seeking highly motivated personnel with current qualifications and accredited tickets with a minimum of 3 years' experience for these roles.
As a Telecommunications Rigger you will be required to take responsibility of the work site and be a team player. Install feeder and antenna systems,
RRU's, TMA's, microwave link installs and upgrades, tower erection and install associated steel work.
All rigging positions require that you have as a minimum:-
Dogging and basic rigging ticket
EME awareness
Tower rescue
Working at heights
EWP above 11m
Construction card
First aid
Full current Australian drivers licence
JD Communications is ISO 9001:2016 accredited and offers excellent rates of pay and conditions.
Positions are on casual, contract or permanent basis. Working away from home may be necessary along with working irregular hours (weekends) when
required.
