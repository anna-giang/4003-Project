The Role:
As our Solution Architect you will provide technical leadership and domain professional expertise related to designing solutions, tools, processes and
technological roadmaps for major cross-domain programs & systems, whilst ensuring alignment to technology and business strategies for Endeavour
Drinks. Leading the development of complex system designs to ensure effective solutions are designed in accordance with business requirements,
standards and technology strategy.
Key Responsibilities will include:
Applying Architecture development and governance processes for specific projects to promote effective quality and compliance within Architecture,
Development of solutions for projects according to architecture guidelines and processes,
Prove new concepts/tools by conducting or coordinating Proofs of Concept,
Contribute to the development, maintenance and application of architecture policies, standards and guidelines,
Facilitating high quality, timely IT estimates for solutions and roadmaps to ensure effective IT estimation of technology solutions,
Ensuring attention to non functional deliverables such as adherence to security, data privacy concerns, performance capabilities, including
scalability of solutions,
Ensuring solutions leverage modern platforms, eg: cloud, tools, techniques and processes.
Investigate and recommend improvement opportunities to drive continuous improvement and ensure remains innovative and up
to date with market leading technologies and industry trends.
Develop and grow relationships with key stakeholders including Heads of Business Unit and Technology General Managers to ensure appropriate
stakeholders are effectively engaged in the formulation of Architecture direction,
Provide technology advice and guidance to Business Unit Heads and Technology General Managers within a single domain to assist the business
in the formulation of business strategy and technology investment decisions,
Strong ability to convey technical information to non-technical business users and leaders.
The Ideal Candidate will possess:
Rich knowledge and experience with retail/e-commerce systems,
Track record of designing complex and modern technology solutions based on business requirements that complement existing and future service,
Solid knowledge and experience of best practice architecture standards, including TOGAF,
Solid understanding of Agile principles and DevOps practices,
Excellent knowledge of Azure cloud offerings, and some knowledge of other Cloud solutions,
A .Net software development background,
A "can do attitude" - takes ownership of the task, is accountable for the result and responsible along the way,
Excellent written and verbal communication skills,
Desire for learning and continuous improvement,
Good knowledge of solution quotation and business case development.
