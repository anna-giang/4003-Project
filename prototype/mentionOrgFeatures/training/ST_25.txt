The Opportunity
As a Test Analyst at TAFE NSW, you will be responsible for ensuring that
testing engagements are successfully carried out, preparing test plans
and a test phase, test cases for a given test phase based on the Test
Objective Matrices, executing and running test cases and other required
tests as directed, raising defects during test execution and following
them through to closure. This role is based within a team of SME,
Technical and Business Analysts and business resources to delivery
testing outcomes.
This is a 12-month temporary full-time opportunity located at Ultimo
TAFE NSW Campus.

Review the Position Description and Information Package for more
information
About You
Ideally, you will will be degree qualified with relevant industry experience
working a Test Analyst across the testing lifecycle: Sprint Testing,
Systems Testing, Data Migration Testing, System Integration Testing and
support of User Acceptance Testing. With experience in testing the
PeopleSoft Campus Solutions platform functionality (Curriculum
management, Student Finance, Enrolments, Reporting, etc.) within an
Educational Domain at Enterprise Level. Solid experience in using JIRA
and Zephyr/JIRA for test management, defects management and
requirements management is required.

You will be resilient and able to work in a fast-paced, complex technical
environment undergoing significant change across people, process and
technology. Your excellent communication skills will enable you to
develop and maintain an effective working relationship within the SMS
Program resources and across ICT departments.

Additional requirements:
1. Experience in use of the Atlassian Suite (Confluence, JIRA and
Bamboo)
2. Strong knowledge of web, mobile and cloud technologies such as
AWS or Azure
3. Experience in testing web services and protocols (HTTP/XML/REST
APIs)
4. Hands on experience using Test Stubs and test automation tools for
functional and load testing
5. Certified Agile Tester (CAT) qualification or equivalent
